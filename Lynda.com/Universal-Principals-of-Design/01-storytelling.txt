DEFINITIONS:
storytelling: 
  - Traditional storytelling is a method of evoking imagery, emotions, and understanding through the presentation of events from a storyteller to an audience. Forms include: oral, textual, graphical, and most recently, digital.
  -  But in design sense, stories can be attached to brands, products, intefaces...virtually anything...and by doing so, they can be made more compelling.

FUNDAMENTAL ELEMENTS THAT ALL COMPELLING STORIES HAVE IN COMMON:
Setting:
  - provides an anchor
  - helps orient the audience by providing a sense of time and place
Characters:
  - Story becomes relevant to an audience through its characters. Interesting characters draw an audience in and keep its attention.
