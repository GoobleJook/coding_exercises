The Flex Container Properties (6)

Flex-direction || Flex-wrap || Flex-flow || Justify-content || Align-items || Align-content

Flex Container Property Values

flex-direction: row || column || row-reverse || column-reverse

flex-wrap: wrap || no-wrap || wrap-reverse

flex-flow: (shorthand property that takes flex-direction and flex-wrap values, i.e. flex-flow: row wrap

justify-content (aligns on main-axis): flex-start || flex-end || center || space-between (same spacing between flex items) || space-around (same spacing around flex items)

align-items (default: stretch -- aligns on cross-axis): flex-start || flex-end || center || stretch || baseline

align-content (used on multi-line flex containers -- default: stretch): stretch || flex-start || flex-end || center ||

The Flex Item Properties

order || flex-grow || flex-shrink || flex-basis

Order: move a flex-item from one position to another, like you would do with "sortable" lists, default: 0; lower values appear first

flex: 0 1 auto; is shorthand for flex-grow: 0; flex-shrink: 1; flex-basis: auto;
