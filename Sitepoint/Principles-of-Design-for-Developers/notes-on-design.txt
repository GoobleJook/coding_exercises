COLOR AND CONTRAST:
Contrast (in regards to color) -- More than just complete opposites; contrast is the visual difference between elements that makes it distinguishable from like elements and the background. It stands out or looks different from the others.

Includes either a difference in either luminensce or the color itself, whether in a graphic or a photo.

Contrast can work just as well, if not better, using contrasts in the same family, not opposites.

Color families: 3 categorized color types that come about by dividing up the color whirl into types:
- warm: red, orange, yellow variations
- cool: green, blue, purple variations
- neutral: black, white, brown variations
